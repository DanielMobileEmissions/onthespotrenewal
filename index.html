What is On The Spot Renewal?

Emissions Test + Registration Renewal — All at Once

On The Spot Renewal (OTS) is Utah's official program that allows authorized stations to process your vehicle registration renewal at the same time as your emissions test — no separate DMV trip required.

As a certified On The Spot Renewal authorized station, MobileEmissions.com brings this entire process directly to your driveway. One appointment. One stop. Walk away fully registered.

Most customers are in and out in under 10 minutes. Your new registration sticker is applied on the spot. We serve Utah County, Davis County, and Cache County.

Common Questions

On The Spot Renewal FAQs

What is On The Spot Renewal in Utah?
On The Spot Renewal is a Utah DMV program that allows authorized emissions stations to process your vehicle registration renewal at the time of your emissions test. Instead of visiting the DMV separately, everything is handled in one appointment.
Is MobileEmissions.com an authorized On The Spot Renewal station?
Yes — MobileEmissions.com is a state-certified On The Spot Renewal authorized station. We can legally process your registration renewal at the time of your emissions test, and we do it at your home or workplace.
Do I still need to go to the DMV?
No. When you book the On The Spot Renewal add-on with us, your registration is renewed on the spot. Your new sticker is applied to your vehicle before we leave. No DMV trip needed.
What counties do you serve for On The Spot Renewal?
We currently serve Utah County, Davis County, and Cache County. Enter your ZIP code above to confirm your address is within our service area.
How long does the emissions test and renewal take?
Most appointments are completed in under 10 minutes. We show up, test your vehicle, process the renewal, apply your new sticker, and you're done.
What if my vehicle fails the emissions test?
If your vehicle doesn't pass, we'll explain the results and next steps. The On The Spot Renewal can only be processed after a passed emissions test, but we'll make sure you understand what's needed.
