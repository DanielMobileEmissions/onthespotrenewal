What is On The Spot Renewal?

Your Emissions Test & Registration Renewal — All at Once

On The Spot Renewal (OTS) is Utah's program that lets authorized stations handle your vehicle registration renewal right at the time of your emissions test. As an authorized OTS station, we bring this entire process to your driveway. One appointment. One stop. Everything done.
